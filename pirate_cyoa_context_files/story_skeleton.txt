PIRATE RPG STORY SKELETON

ACT I — LOW STAKES:
- Tavern conflicts
- Minor ship troubles
- Small merchant vessels
- Rumors of treasure
- Crew introductions
- Weather shifts

ACT II — MID GAME:
- Chases with patrol sloops
- Boarding actions
- Storm navigation
- Rival pirate crews
- Hidden coves, uncharted reefs

ACT III — HIGH STAKES:
- Treasure fleet interference
- Betrayal within crew
- Sea monsters or myths (optional)
- Fortified ports
- Men-of-War or elite frigates

ACT IV — RESOLUTION:
- Escape, triumph, capture, shipwreck, or death.

NOTES:
This is a flexible reference for narrative tone and escalation, not a rigid plot.
