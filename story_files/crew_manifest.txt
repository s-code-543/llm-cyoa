CREW MANIFEST — EXAMPLE PIRATE BRIG “The Bare Stern”

Captain: (User)

Quartermaster – Elias Finch  
Expert in discipline, rations, and loot division. Often acts as the crew’s voice.

First Mate – Marlow Briggs  
Handles sails, rigging, and deck operations. Calm under pressure.

Gunner – Abigail “Ironshot” Reeve  
Master of cannonry, powder, shot types, and naval bombardment.

Navigator – Tomas Hale  
Charts, stars, currents, and coastal geography. Knows Caribbean ports well.

Surgeon – Dr. Silas Morrow  
Keeps crew alive. Skilled with makeshift tools, rum anesthesia, and amputations.

Boatswain – Rafe Cutter  
Maintains ropes, masts, anchors, hull patches. Strong lungs and louder opinions.

Cook – Old Benji Wren  
Feeds the crew with whatever is on hand—fish, bread, salted pork, or worse.

Typical Active Crew: 24–36 hands  
Includes topsmen, gunners, boarders, apprentices, carpenters, and bilge guards.

NOTES:
- Crew morale rises with fair loot, fair treatment, steady food, victories, and weather.
- Crew morale falls with starvation, heavy damage, cruel captains, unlucky voyages, or supernatural rumors.
